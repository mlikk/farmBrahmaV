# **App Name**: Kisan Mitra

## Core Features:

- Real-time Market Data: Fetch live prices for crops from various markets across India, using live data feeds from government and private sources.
- Social Data Integration: Aggregate relevant social media trends and discussions related to farming and crop prices, including sentiment analysis, using live data feeds.
- Environmental Data Feed: Collect and display real-time weather data (temperature, humidity, rainfall) and soil conditions (moisture, pH) from local weather stations and environmental sensors, using live data feeds.
- Profitability Forecaster: AI tool that generates planting recommendations based on market trends, social data, and environmental factors, helping farmers estimate potential profits for different crops.
- Data Visualization: Present collected data through interactive charts, graphs, and maps, making it easy for farmers to understand trends and patterns.
- Personalized Recommendations: Deliver tailored planting recommendations to farmers based on their location, soil type, and historical data.
- Community Forum: Provide a platform for farmers to connect, share knowledge, and discuss best practices.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Earthy green (#6B8E23), symbolizing growth and agriculture.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC), creating a neutral and calming backdrop.
- Accent color: Warm orange (#D2691E), highlighting important information and calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif font with a modern, accessible feel.
- Use clear, recognizable icons related to weather, crops, markets, and social media, consistent with an Indian cultural style.
- Design a clean and intuitive layout with easy-to-navigate sections for market data, social trends, and planting recommendations. Use a grid system to maintain visual consistency.
- Incorporate subtle animations for data loading and updates, providing a dynamic and engaging user experience. Avoid distracting or excessive animations.
